Neapolitan Pizza

By User

Neapolitan Pizza is a classic Italian pizza known for its thin, soft, and chewy crust. Topped with simple and high-quality ingredients, it's a favorite among pizza enthusiasts..

Ingredients:

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Toppings:

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions:

For the Pizza Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center.
  3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well. Stir until the dough comes together.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it's smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.

For the Pizza Sauce:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat a little olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens. Let it cool.

Assembling and Baking:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 500-550°F or 260-290°C) with a pizza stone inside if available.
  2. Divide the dough into 2-4 equal portions (depending on your desired pizza size). Roll out each portion into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Place the rolled dough on a piece of parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce on the dough.
  5. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil.
  7. Use a pizza peel or a baking sheet to transfer the pizza (with parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone in the oven.
  8. Bake for about 7-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a moment, and then slice and serve.

Neapolitan Pizza is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven with its signature thin, soft, and blistered crust. Enjoy your homemade Neapolitan pizza!
